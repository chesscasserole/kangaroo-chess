Kangaroo Chess Rules:
• Standard chess rules apply
• On your turn, you may swap any two of your own pieces instead of moving
• Swapping counts as your full turn
• You cannot swap with opponent's pieces
• Click "Swap Mode" then select two of your pieces to swap
